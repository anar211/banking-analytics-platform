# Business Insights

## Project Goal

The objective of this project is to analyze customer data from a banking marketing campaign and identify factors influencing term deposit subscriptions.

---

# Key Business Questions

1. Which customer segments are most likely to subscribe?

2. Does age influence subscription probability?

3. Which occupations have the highest conversion rate?

4. Does account balance affect customer behavior?

5. Which communication channel performs better?

6. Does campaign duration impact success?

7. Which months generate the highest conversion?

8. Which customers should be targeted in future campaigns?

---

# Expected Business Outcomes

- Improve marketing efficiency
- Increase campaign conversion rate
- Reduce customer acquisition cost
- Better customer segmentation
- Support executive decision-making
- Improve customer lifetime value

---

# Potential KPIs

- Deposit Conversion Rate
- Campaign Success Rate
- Average Account Balance
- Customer Segmentation
- Revenue per Customer
- Marketing ROI
